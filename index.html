Our Story

A Taste of Vietnam,
Right Here in Surrey

Welcome to Chubby Banh Mi & Bubble Tea, where every bite tells a story of tradition, passion, and family recipes passed down through generations. What started as a small family dream has blossomed into Surrey's beloved destination for authentic Vietnamese flavors.

Our banh mi starts with baguettes baked fresh every morning—crispy on the outside, soft and airy inside. We pile them high with house-marinated proteins, tangy pickled vegetables, fresh herbs, and our secret sauces that have customers coming back for more.
